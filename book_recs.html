[Current] Favorite Books

  1. Stories of Your Life & Others, Ted Chiang

    2. Category: Sci-Fi, Short-Story

    If you like black mirror or enjoy stretching your mind beyond the world we know, read this. Exhalations is the second book in this series (if one can even call it that) & it is also worth the read!

  2. String Theory, David Foster Wallace

    3. Category: Non-Fiction, Short-Story

    This is the anthology of all DFW works about tennis.

  3. How the Universe got it's Spots: a Diary of Finite Time in a Finite Space, Janna Levin

    4. Category: Non-Fiction

    This is the most beautifully written astrophysics book I've ever read. The weaving of her own personal journey with that of her work (researching the shape of the universe) engages the reader into becoming both intellectually & emotionally involved.

  4. Franny & Zooey, JD Salinger

    5. Category: Fiction, Short-Story

    This is my most read book by far. Maybe I come back to it only from nostalgia at this point, but I think Salinger's genius is much better seen through the Glass family than Catcher in the Rye.

  5. Anna Karenina

    6. Category: Fiction

    My favorite plotline in this book is that of Levin. Unfortunately, this was entirely skipped in the movie.
